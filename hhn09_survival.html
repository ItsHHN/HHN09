Arrive early to the event and CHOOSE YOUR FATE once you walk through the front gates.

1) Travel to your RIGHT down Rodeo Dr. to Hollywood Blvd. and experience:
• Lights, Camera, Hacktion
• Horrorwood Die In
• War of The Living Dead

You will have to visit the one and only Cirque Du Freak if you want to experence:
• Chucky - Friends til the End
• Silver Screams
Then onward to the sewers to The Spawning.

OR

2) Travel STRAIGHT down Production Central through Containment and jump right into some House Attractions:
• The Wolfman
• Dracula - Legacy of Blood
To experience Frankenstein - Creation of the Damned you must brave Apoclypse: City of Cannibals

Continue on and you will have the chance to visit Sam Meetz butcher shop in Leave it to Cleaver or see the Rocky Horror Picture Show Tribute. Finally going down the dark long road to SAW.

The evening’s first and last performances of Bill & Ted’s Excellent Halloween Adventure are the least crowded.

You can reduce the time you wait in line with a Halloween Horror Nights Express Pass.

Florida Residents can spend their last few hours of sanity before descending into the chaos of Halloween Horror Nights with edge-of-your-seat thrills of Universal's Islands of Adventure with an Islands of Adventure $15 Upgrade ticket.

Go behind the screams even further with the NEW "Unmasking the Horror" Tour.
